The One Girl I Pursued

I saw her first across the night,
A spark of hope, a fleeting sight.
Her gaze, a world I couldn’t deny,
A silent promise beneath the sky.

I followed trails that she had tread,
Each word she spoke stayed in my head.
Through whispers soft and moments few,
I knew my heart would chase her too.

I braved the storms, the quiet fear,
Believing love would draw me near.
And every smile that she would give,
Made me certain, love must live.

She walked with grace, so unaware,
Of how my soul pursued her there.
I watched her dance in sunlit streams,
The one who lived inside my dreams.

I wrote her name on stars above,
A whispered prayer, a vow of love.
With every step, I hoped she knew,
That all I did was to pursue.

The days grew long, the nights stretched thin,
Yet still I fought to let love in.
Her laugh became the song I’d sing,
A beacon bright through everything.

And when she smiled, the world was bright,
Her voice could turn the dark to light.
A simple glance, and I was through—
Forever bound to her I knew.

Now here we stand, no more to chase,
She smiles at me with quiet grace.
The road was long, but dreams came true—
At last, I found the one I pursued.

— Written with every step of the journey in my mind, -CARY
